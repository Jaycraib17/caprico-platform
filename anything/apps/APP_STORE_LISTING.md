## App Store Review Notes

### Testing Instructions
1. Launch the app - it will show a splash screen followed by onboarding
2. Skip onboarding or complete it
3. Browse jobs on the Home tab (jobs are loaded from our production database)
4. Tap any job to view full details
5. Test save/unsave functionality (requires sign-in)
6. Navigate to Companies tab to view company profiles  
7. Test filters modal by tapping filter icon
8. All features work without sign-in except saving jobs and creating alerts
