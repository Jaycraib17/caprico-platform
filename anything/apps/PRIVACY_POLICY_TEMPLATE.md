## Data We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, password (encrypted), first name, last name
- **Job Preferences**: Your country, preferred job categories, experience level, employment types, timezone
- **Saved Jobs & Companies**: Jobs and companies you bookmark
- **Job Alerts**: Custom search filters you save for email notifications
- **Application Tracking**: Job applications you track (company name, job title, status, notes)
- **Support Tickets**: Information you provide when contacting customer support

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Analytics**: Pages viewed, features used, time spent in app, device type, operating system
- **Purchase Data**: In-app purchase transaction IDs, purchase dates, product IDs (processed via RevenueCat)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your resume content
- We do not collect your social security number or government ID
- We do not collect precise location data (only country-level)
- We do not track you across other apps or websites

## How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:
- Show you relevant remote job opportunities based on your country and preferences
- Send you email alerts when new jobs match your saved searches (only if you opt in)
- Send you push notifications for job matches (only if you opt in)
- Process in-app purchases for premium services
- Respond to your support requests
- Improve our app and services through anonymous usage analytics
- Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

## Your Rights & Choices

### Access Your Data
You can view and download your data by emailing support@capriremote.com with the subject "Data Access Request"

### Delete Your Data
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by:
1. Emailing support@capriremote.com with the subject "Delete My Account"
2. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days

### Revoke Consent
You can revoke consent for data collection at any time by:
- Disabling push notifications in Settings → Notifications
- Disabling email notifications in Settings → Notifications
- Deleting your account (see above)

### Opt Out of Analytics
You can opt out of analytics tracking by:
- iOS: Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements → Share iPhone Analytics (disable)
- Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Job Alerts & Saved Jobs**: Retained while your account is active
- **Purchase Records**: Retained for 7 years (required for tax/legal compliance)
- **Support Tickets**: Retained for 3 years
- **Analytics Data**: Anonymized and retained for 2 years
- **Deleted Accounts**: All data permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request

## Data Security

We protect your data using:
- Industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit
- Encrypted password storage (bcrypt/argon2)
- Secure cloud infrastructure (AWS/Google Cloud/etc.)
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls limiting who can view your data
